Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

My first experiences playing with code were at a young age, playing on a programmable computer. Since then, I've found myself returning to the blend of logic and creativity that coding requires, initially as an interest in seeing if I could do it, and since as an exploration of what can be done with code.

The more I learn, the more excited I become about seeing what else can be done. Building sites, seeing them take shape, adding more interactivity and creating webapps all energize me. The more I practice, the more I am fascinated with HTML, CSS, and programming languages. I look forward to producing viable, beneficial products for small businesses that are responsive, interactive, and accessible to people in many walks of life.
